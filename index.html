Young Reads


About us:

We are undergraduate students from University Malaya, and are currently assigned a SULAM project under Thinking and Communication Skills (WIX2001). Our project is Young Reads, where we will work with primary schools and hold story reading sessions for their pupils. Furthermore, we would like to seek for collaborators from the corporate world to bring this project to greater heights. Our objectives are:

    -To instil the habit of reading among primary school students
    -To train students with the skill of summarizing and/or writing reflections based on text material

For our collaborators:

Dear Sir / Madam,

We are second year undergraduate students from University Malaya. We are currently undertaking Thinking and Communication Skills (WIX2001) course this semester and the course requires us to conduct a Service-Learning Malaysia (SULAM) project. ...Our project is a reading campaign called ‘Young Reads’. We proudly to invite your students in our upcoming event. Our project is Young Reads, where we will work together with the students and hold story reading sessions, do a summary writing session and impromptu story telling competition.

A few words about our project: We realized that COVID-19 on top of digital normalization has made the current generation almost devoid of books and text material, as they spend most of their time on social media or games. Besides the latter software undoubtedly providing better stimuli and enjoyment to their users compared to books, the absence of face-to-face education further diminishes the presence of books in the lives of children today. Therefore, Young Reads aims to rekindle the interest of reading in children by going out of our way in this busy university life to read meaningful stories to them. Moreover, we hope to improve the reading, listening, and thinking skills of the students through this event.

Having that said, we would like to humbly request for your support as we organise this event. We are plainly undergraduates, but with your assistance, the odds of achieving wonders might not be that feeble after all.

Quoting the worlds from King Solomon, the wisest king that ever lived, "Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old, he will not depart from it". We believe there is a necessity to instil the habit of reading in children of this generation, and we want to contribute to this necessity as much as we can. Your reply is much anticipated, thank you for reading this message.
